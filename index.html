Rich Dad Poor Dad

Robert T. Kiyosaki

"Rich Dad Poor Dad" revolves around financial education and the lessons the author, Robert T. Kiyosaki, learned from his two fathers. It emphasizes the importance of financial literacy, investment, and building wealth through assets. The book uses anecdotes and practical advice to encourage readers to think differently about money and wealth accumulation.
This book has been influential for many individuals seeking to enhance their financial intelligence and adopt a more proactive approach to managing their finances.
